Dr. Michal Johnson, DMD, PA

“Dentistry is my passion. It gives me the ability to use the gift of my mind to apply my knowledge, and the gift of my heart to treat my patients with compassion and give them all bright smiles.

Over the past 15 years, dentistry has given me the perfect balance with my professional and personal life. Most of my patients who come to see me for the first time have experienced dental anxiety, and I pride myself on the fact that by the time they leave, they are no longer afraid of the dentist. I like interacting with my patients, not only by providing dentistry in a caring and comfortable manner, but also by discussing in detail how to maintain their dental health so that they can retain their own teeth for a lifetime.”

– Dr. Johnson

Commitment to Education

A firm believer in providing quality care, Dr. Michal Johnson went to the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill to complete his Advanced Education in General Dentistry after completing his Doctorate of Dental Surgery. Since then, Dr. Johnson has completed over 450 hours of continuing education courses to keep up with the latest advancements in dental technology. He is a member of the American Dental Association, the Academy of General Dentistry, and the Hawaii Dental Association.

Outside the Office

When Dr. Johnson is not in the office, he enjoys spending time with his family and playing with his three grand children, the youngest of whom is six months old. His hobbie is reading. He is also active in the community and involved with ensuring quality education for children.
